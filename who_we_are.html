WHO WE ARE

Himalayan Bio Organic Foods Pvt Ltd. is sister concern of Sarveshwar Foods Limited. Sarveshwar Group was established by Anand Katariya in 1999. It was under his able guidance that My father gave the new dimension and vision to the company. It is a family owned and operated business that is committed to provide finest quality rice for your family. Since 1995 Sarveshwar family has been serving healthy, great tasting rice while respecting and sustaining the earth. Today, the fourth generation carries on the family heritage by using eco-positive farming methods that produce wholesome, healthful products while improving and protecting the environment for generations to come. With over 50 years of experience in sourcing and processing of Rice, Sarveshwar has introduced Organic Products from the virgin lands of Jammu & Kashmir in India. The core pillars of our groups are the quality, authenticity, and purity of our products that sets us apart, as a reliable organic product manufacturer.

At ORGANIC, we believe in the philosophy of “SATVIK” conscious lifestyle. We have our lands in the foothills of Himalayas and our produce is nourished by fertile mineral-rich soil, organic manure and snow melted waters of river Chenab, without the use of artificial fertilizers and chemicals which are then sold through a household brand name ORGANIC who believes in spreading positive and pure energy. Each of the product is hygienically processed and packed and undergo multiple rounds of quality assurance to retain its natural essence and freshness throughout the shelf life.

Organic Products are ultimate blend of taste, aroma and purity cultivated under strict supervision of experts and after following Good Agriculture Practices. Our products are undoubtly the mark of superiority and sanctity because we at Organic believe in nurturing relationship that will last forever.
