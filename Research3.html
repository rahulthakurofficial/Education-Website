Electric Vehicle Project

Innovative Research on Electric Vehicles at IIT Bombay

Project Overview

The Electric Vehicle project at IIT Bombay aims to develop sustainable and efficient electric vehicle technologies. This project encompasses battery technology, motor design, and energy management systems to create state-of-the-art electric vehicles.

Objectives

  • Develop advanced battery technologies.
  • Design efficient electric motors.
  • Create effective energy management systems.
  • Collaborate with automotive industry partners.
  • Publish research in top-tier journals.
