IIT Bombay

About Us

Our Mission

Established in 1958, the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) stands as a beacon of academic excellence, innovation, and technological advancement in India. Situated in the bustling city of Mumbai, IIT Bombay has evolved into one of the premier institutes of higher learning not only in India but also on the global stage. With its steadfast commitment to fostering a conducive environment for learning, research, and innovation, IIT Bombay continues to produce world-class engineers, scientists, and entrepreneurs who contribute significantly to society.

History

The journey of IIT Bombay began with a vision to nurture the burgeoning talent pool in India and to provide them with world-class education in engineering and technology. Since its inception, the institute has witnessed remarkable growth and development, both in terms of infrastructure and academic excellence. Over the years, IIT Bombay has expanded its academic programs, research initiatives, and industry collaborations, solidifying its position as a leader in higher education and research.

Our Alumni

The alumni of IIT Bombay form a formidable network of leaders, innovators, and change-makers who have made significant contributions to various fields, both in India and abroad. From pioneering research in academia to leading multinational corporations, IIT Bombay alumni continue to leave an indelible mark on society through their exemplary achievements and contributions. Moreover, many alumni actively engage with the institute through mentorship programs, guest lectures, and philanthropic initiatives, further strengthening the bond between the alma mater and its graduates.
