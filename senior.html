Branches Offered at IIT Bombay

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Focuses on the study of electronic systems, communication technologies, and related areas.

Civil Engineering

Involves the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, and buildings.

Food Processing Technology

Focuses on the processing, preservation, and packaging of food products.

Mechanical Engineering

Covers the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.

Electrical Engineering

Involves the study of electrical systems, circuits, and devices.

Agricultural Engineering

Focuses on the application of engineering principles to agricultural processes and machinery.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Combines electrical engineering with electronics to study integrated circuits, microcontrollers, and more.

Computer Science and Engineering

Covers the principles of computing, programming, and system design.

Chemical Engineering

Focuses on the process of converting raw materials into valuable products through chemical processes.
