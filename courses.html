Workshops

Various workshops are organized throughout the academic year to provide hands-on experience and practical knowledge in different areas of study.

Previous workshops have covered topics such as coding, robotics, entrepreneurship, etc.

Guest Lectures

Eminent personalities from various industries and academia are invited to deliver guest lectures to students.

These lectures cover a wide range of topics including career guidance, industry trends, and academic insights.

Seminars

Seminars are conducted on relevant topics to enhance the knowledge and skills of students.

These seminars often feature expert speakers and facilitate discussions on current issues and advancements in various fields.

Exhibitions

Exhibitions are organized to showcase the talents, projects, and innovations of students.

These exhibitions provide a platform for students to display their work and interact with peers and industry professionals.

Quiz Competitions

Quiz competitions are conducted to promote healthy competition and enhance knowledge retention among students.

These competitions cover a wide range of topics and encourage active participation from students.
