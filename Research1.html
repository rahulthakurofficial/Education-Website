VLSI Project

Innovations in Very-Large-Scale Integration at IIT Bombay

Project Overview

The VLSI (Very-Large-Scale Integration) project at IIT Bombay aims to push the boundaries of integrated circuit design and fabrication. Our research focuses on developing advanced techniques for designing, testing, and manufacturing high-performance, energy-efficient VLSI circuits.

Objectives

  • Design and implementation of low-power VLSI circuits.
  • Exploration of new materials and technologies for VLSI fabrication.
  • Development of innovative testing methodologies for VLSI systems.
  • Collaboration with industry partners to transfer technology to real-world applications.
